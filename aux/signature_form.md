We are seeking your endoresment in securing funding from the R Consortium ISC Grant Program to support the development of HydraR, a high-performance agentic infrastructure for reproducible scientific research. HydraR aims to address a critical gap in the R ecosystem: the lack of reproducible infrastructure for Large Language Model (LLM) orchestration. As AI agents increasingly assist in scientific code generation and data analysis, HydraR ensures these "agentic journeys" are stateful, auditable, and isolated, maintaining the rigorous standards required for modern bioinformatics and statistics. Your support on this project will help in the foundational AI readiness of the R community, and in empowering researchers to leverage AI safely, transparently, and at the scale required for national infrastructure.
